FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Am I eligible to join TXE?
All undergraduate students currently enrolled at the University of Michigan are eligible to apply to TXE. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, age, marital status, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, gender expression, disability, religion, height, weight, or veteran status.

TXE administers its application process at the beginning of the Fall and Winter semesters. Refer to the Apply page for more specific information about our recruitment process and timeline.
What's the expected time commitment?
40 hours every day.
Why should I join?
TXE offers a wide range of professional and social opportunities to development oneself in an occupational and personal sense.
Also check out our upcoming events to see some of the amazing events we have planned for the upcoming semester, and refer to our members page to see some of their stories.
Does TXE only take engineering majors?
No! We encourage every student who is interested in TXE to apply, regardless of their major. While we are a tech-based professional organization, we firmly believe technology depends on the perspectives of individuals from every field of study, and strive to cultivate such an environment within our membership.
More questions?
If you have any questions that weren't answered here, feel free to reach out to any of our members, or send us an email at txe.executive@umich.edu! We will be sure to get back to you ASAP!
