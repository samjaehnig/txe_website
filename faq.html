FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Am I eligible to join TXE?
Any student that is currently enrolled at the University of Michigan is eligible to apply to TXE.
We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, age, marital status, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, gender expression, disability, religion, height, weight, or veteran status.
The application process commences at the beginning of the Fall and Winter semesters. Refer to the Apply page for more information.
What's the expected time commitment?
40 hours every day.
Why should I join?
TXE offers a wide range of professional and social opportunities to development oneself in an occupational and personal sense.
Also check out our upcoming events to see some of the amazing events we have planned for the upcoming semester, and refer to our members page to see some of their stories.
Does TXE only take engineering majors?
No! We strongly encourage students from all colleges to apply to TXE. We believe technology relies on individuals from all fields of study.
More questions?
If you have any questions that weren't answered here, feel free to reach out to any of our members, send us an email at txe.executive@umich.edu! We check all of our platforms regularly, and will be sure to get back to you ASAP!
