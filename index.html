ABOUT

Tau Chi Epsilon (TXE), est. 2020 at the University of Michigan, is a professional organization aimed to nurture exceptional leaders in technology. Our mission is to bring resources and guidance to remarkable and well-rounded individuals with a great interest in technology. We are focused on promoting a welcoming, supportive, and collaborative environment where members can form valuable connections, grow holistically, and seize opportunities.
